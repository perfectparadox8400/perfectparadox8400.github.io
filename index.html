About Us

We are Perfect Paradox Team 8400, an FTC team located in Cedar City, Utah. As a First Tech Challenge team through 4-H, we strive to spread our engineering and programming knowledge with our local STEM community. In addition, we compete in FIRST Robotics competitions, and excersise gracious professionalism throughout our team and those around us.
