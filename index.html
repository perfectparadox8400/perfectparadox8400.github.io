About Us

We are Perfect Paradox 8400, a First Tech Challenge robotics team located in Cedar City, Utah. Our group, ages 12-18, and is dual affilated with the non-profit organizations: Lion's Club, and 4-H. As a team, we design, construct, program, and compete with our robot each season. We also strive to bring our knowledge to our community through service activities, and community outreach events.

2025-2026 Challenge: "Decode"

Last year’s FTC challenge, Into the Deep℠, was themed around underwater exploration. Robots scored points by moving and placing “samples” into an observation chamber. Teams also earned points by transporting game pieces and parking in specific zones. The game encouraged creativity, teamwork, and precise robot design.
